Protein Binding Site Prediction from Simulation Data 2023 – 2025
DOI


  • Served as the lead researcher and the first author for the project, designing and performing the core experiments, analyzing data, and preparing the manuscript.

  • Performed rigid & flexible MD simulations and solvation mapping on 34 protein targets and analyzed solvation thermodynamic and interaction features over 80,000 frames of each simulations with the statistical approach.

  • Developed Python-based methodologies to analyze the local solvation environment over the simulation trajectory frames; side chain flipping states, water-protein h-bond interaction, number of water neighbors.

    • Python library used:
      • Pnandas Numpy Scipy Matplotlib MDTraj pymol multiprocessing


Water-Based Pharmacophore 2021 – 2024

  • Performed virtual screening 100K+ DUD-E database against our water-based pharmacophore hypotheses, resulting in screening data comparable to that of ligand-based pharmacophore searches.

  • Used OpenEye Tools and Schrödinger Suites for the analysis and data processing.

  • Developed an automation script in Python for hydrogen bond analysis and PyMOL visualization sessions, which improved project efficiency.

    • Python library used:
      • Pnandas Numpy Scipy Matplotlib MDTraj pymol


Development of Online Repository of Sars-Cov2 (2020)
Github

  • Participated in developing publicly available solvation thermodynamic and structural maps of SARS-CoV-2 targets as a resource to the academic and industrial drug design community to aid in their pursuit of identifying small molecule treatments for COVID-1.

  • Generated visualizations of the GIST and SSTMap on the targets for publications.


Water Binding Residence Time 2019 - 2020

  • Investigated factors to facilitate the breaking of protein-water hydrogen bonds to correlate small molecule ligand residence time and adjacent water dynamics.

  • Developed Python-based to quantify the residence time of water and the percentage of protein-water and water-water hydrogen bonds during the simulation time.

    • Python library used:
      • Pnandas Numpy Scipy Matplotlib MDTraj pymol multiprocessing

Identifying Opioid Receptor Specificity 2019 - 2020

  • To reduce the side effects caused by the opioid medications by increasing the specificity of the three types (mu-, delta-, and kappa) of opioid receptors, applied solvation mapping to molecular docking to enhance enrichment of ligand binding.
